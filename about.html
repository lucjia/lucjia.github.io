About

I'm currently a senior studying Computer Science and Entrepreneurship at the University of Washington, with a focus on accessibility! This fall, I'm applying for PhD programs in HCI and accessible technology. Despite being born and raised in the greater Seattle area, I don't drink much coffee... but I do like the rain sometimes.

In my free time, I enjoy baking, skating, taking long walks around the city (most recently NYC!), hiking, and exploring the PNW. I've been baking since middle school and love making artistic cakes and cookies! You can see more of my work on my informal Instagram portfolio.
